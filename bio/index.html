My name is Drew Powers, and I’m a 23.1-year-old artist from Orlando, FL.

I went to school for studio art. My focus was in painting, but I’m also trained in drawing, printmaking, art history, and a little bit of book production. In between classes I have worked as a freelance graphic designer and web coder for different companies and individuals around Orlando. For the past year, I've worked at Kammel+Morgan Design Group doing the same things.

I am equally fascinated with aesthetics and efficiency.

I believe in the harmony of form and function, and I believe beauty exists just as much in the internal mechanisms of the device as its casing. To me, “design” encompasses a lot more than mindlessly making shapes in Photoshop, and when making websites my code is just as meticulously drafted as the visuals that adorn it.

I take a personal interest in my clients and the work I do. Whatever questions you may have, I’d be glad to answer.
